About Us, Innovatronix

Innovatronix Inc. is a Filipino technology company with a diverse portfolio that spans electronics design and assembly, photography and imaging, power electronics, electric vehicles, and aquaculture farming.


Our Story

Experiencess that shaped our legacy of excellence


1987

Foundation

Innovatronix was founded by Ramon Castillo, a former senior engineer at Intel. Beginning as a small electronics company, it focused on innovative electronics work for major firms such as Intel, Motorola, and Telefunken.

1991

First Major Breakthrough

Innovatronix was commissioned to design and build the electronic scoreboards for the Southeast Asian (SEA) Games held in Manila.
This project marked a turning point, establishing Innovatronix as a trusted leader in electronic display systems.

90s

Expansion Into Consumer Electronics

With growing demand, Innovatronix introduced the TRONIX brand, launching a line of consumer electronic products including:

  • Power-On-Delay (POD10)
  • Electronic ballasts
  • Automatic voltage regulators (AVRs)
  • Air-conditioner controllers

These products soon became common in Filipino households.

1997

Resilience During the Asian Financial Crisis

Despite economic challenges, the company continued innovating. Ramon Castillo recognized the rise of digital imaging and prepared to enter a new industry.
