Energy Savers & Appliance Protectors

These products are designed for easy use, coupled with significant functions that could spell big savings

Zeal 2.0 (12Vdc Mini UPS)

A compact uninterruptible power supply designed to keep your router or modem running for up to 4 hours during power outages, ensuring continuous internet connectivity.

Appliance Timer

An energy-saving device that automatically turns off appliances to prevent power surge damage, reduce electricity costs, and enhance home safety.

Ref Clock Saver

An electronic controller that optimizes refrigerator operation cycles to cut power consumption by nearly half while maintaining consistent cooling performance.

Aircon Timer

An energy-saving timer for window-type air conditioners (up to 3HP) with programmable on/off settings and a built-in fan outlet for added comfort.

Automatic Night Light

A sensor-based lighting controller that automatically turns lights on at dusk and off at dawn, compatible with CFL and LED bulbs up to 80 watts.

Power on Delay

A protection device that delays power restoration to appliances after brownouts, allowing voltage stabilization and preventing compressor damage in refrigerators, TVs, and audio systems.
